# 🛒 BlinkIT Sales Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built using **Microsoft Excel** to analyze BlinkIT's sales performance, outlet performance, customer satisfaction, and product trends.

This project demonstrates how Excel can be used as a Business Intelligence tool to generate actionable insights for decision-making.

# 📌 Business Problem

BlinkIT operates multiple grocery outlets across different locations and outlet types. The management team requires an interactive dashboard to monitor business performance, identify top-performing products and outlets, analyze customer ratings, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze outlet performance across locations and sizes
- Identify best-performing product categories
- Track customer satisfaction using ratings
- Provide actionable business insights and recommendations
- Build an interactive dashboard for management reporting

# ❓ Business Questions Answered

This dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

- Which product category generates the highest sales?
- Which outlet type performs the best?
- Which outlet location contributes the highest revenue?
- What is the sales contribution of Low Fat vs Regular products?
- Which outlet size performs the best?
- How do customer ratings vary across outlet types?
- Which business areas require improvement?
- Which product categories should receive additional inventory?

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Fruits & Vegetables is the highest-selling product category.
- Low Fat products contribute approximately **65%** of total sales.
- Tier 3 outlets generate the highest overall revenue.
- Medium-sized outlets outperform Small and High-sized outlets in sales.
- Grocery Stores achieve the highest average customer rating.
- Supermarket Type 1 contributes the largest share of total sales.
- A few product categories contribute the majority of overall revenue.

---

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

- Expand operations in Tier 3 locations.
- Increase inventory for high-performing product categories.
- Promote low-selling categories through marketing campaigns.
- Improve operational performance of lower-performing outlet types.
- Focus on maintaining availability of Low Fat products.
- Continue monitoring customer ratings to improve customer satisfaction.
